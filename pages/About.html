About....


Mawlai is a Census Town city in district of East Khasi Hills, Meghalaya. The Mawlai Census Town has population of 55,012 of which 26,335 are males while 28,677 are females as per report released by Census India 2011.

Population of Children with age of 0-6 is 8542 which is 15.53 % of total population of Mawlai (CT). In Mawlai Census Town, Female Sex Ratio is of 1089 against state average of 989. Moreover Child Sex Ratio in Mawlai is around 936 compared to Meghalaya state average of 970. Literacy rate of Mawlai city is 91.92 % higher than state average of 74.43 %. In Mawlai, Male literacy is around 93.05 % while female literacy rate is 90.91 %.

Mawlai Census Town has total administration over 10,661 houses to which it supplies basic amenities like water and sewerage. It is also authorize to build roads within Census Town limits and impose taxes on properties coming under its jurisdiction.

In Mawlai (CT), most of the (CT) population is from Schedule Tribe (ST). Schedule Tribe (ST) constitutes 96.90 % while Schedule Caste (SC) were 0.39 % of total population in Mawlai (CT).

Out of total population, 19,543 were engaged in work or business activity. Of this 11,784 were males while 7,759 were females. In census survey, worker is defined as person who does business, job, service, and cultivator and labour activity. Of total 19543 working population, 93.70 % were engaged in Main Work while 6.30 % of total workers were engaged in Marginal Work.
